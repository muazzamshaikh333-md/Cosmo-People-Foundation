Nashik, Maharashtra

Transforming Lives with Awareness, Action & Empowerment

Cosmo People Foundation organized a dynamic awareness event on June 8, 2025, focused on multiple social themes including *organ donation*, *women’s safety*, and *youth empowerment*. With an inspiring message of “Changing Mindsets, Creating Futures”, the event brought together students, professionals, social workers, and community leaders from across Nashik.

Highlights of the Event

  • Organ donation awareness drive and registration counters
  • Self-defense workshops for women and girls
  • Motivational talk on leadership and self-growth by Rimay SK
  • Tree plantation and clean city initiative in partnership with Nashik Municipal Corporation
  • Health checkup camp and eye donation pledge corner

Mission & Vision

At Cosmo People Foundation, our mission is to uplift communities through social responsibility, education, and sustainable impact. This event was a reflection of our continuous efforts to bridge societal gaps, empower the underprivileged, and create an inclusive, thriving future.
