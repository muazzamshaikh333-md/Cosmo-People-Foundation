Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

Cosmo People Foundation (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the website www.cosmopeople.in (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our services, including through our website, donation forms, and lead campaigns on platforms like Facebook and Instagram (Meta).

1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information through direct interaction and automated technologies:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, city, state, country, organization name, or any other details you provide via forms or communication.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Your IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, time spent on our site, and usage data collected via cookies and tracking technologies (see Section 5).

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Process volunteer registrations, event signups, or donations
  • Send updates, newsletters, and impact stories (only with your consent)
  • Improve our website, services, and engagement strategies
  • Analyze user behavior for marketing and optimization
  • Comply with applicable laws and protect against fraud or misuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We never sell your data. We may share it only with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors for web hosting, analytics, marketing, or customer support.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or government request.
  • Business Changes: In the event of a merger or transfer of assets related to the foundation.

4. Meta Lead Ads & Third-Party Platforms

If you submit your data via Facebook or Instagram lead ads, your information is processed under Meta’s privacy terms and our data handling policies. We use this data strictly to offer relevant services like volunteering, donation support, or campaign updates. Meta may also use your data according to their privacy policy, which is outside our control. You may update or remove your data by emailing us or adjusting your privacy settings on the Meta platform.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for personalization, performance, and analytics. These include third-party analytics and advertising partners. You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings at any time. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability (where applicable)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (if based on consent)
  • Request restriction or objection to processing (where applicable)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email listed below.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no online system is entirely secure. Please use strong passwords and avoid sharing sensitive data unnecessarily online.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time to reflect legal, technological, or operational changes. We recommend reviewing it periodically. The date of the latest update will always be mentioned at the top.
